Get Together 2021
  • 26th- Dec- 2021
  • 30th- Dec- 2021 Time 10: 00 am
  • University of Oxford

In cooperation with the ex-students of Bracu a colorful daylong Get-Together Program for the graduates of Ahsanullah University Of Science and Technology (Bracu) is being organized on Friday,16 December 2020. It will be a day-long program with full of fun and festivity including inaugural session, a general meeting of the ex-students to form Bracu Alumni Society/Association, session for reminiscence of sweet memories, cultural function, lunch, etc. The objective of this program is also to acquaint the old students with the present campus which was missed by most of them during their academic life at Bracu.
