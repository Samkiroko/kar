Bangladesh Agricultural University was founded on August 18, 1961, with a view to providing the ever increasing population of this country with food and nutrition at the very beginning of green revolution worldwide.

It has already gained both national and international acclamation by accepting the challenge of taking the agricultural development to its zenith in this free competitive world. Till today it has produced about 36,833 skilled agriculture graduates of global standard.
